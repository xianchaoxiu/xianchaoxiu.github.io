About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Associate Professor at the Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I received my Ph.D in Mathematics from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2019, and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University. I was a Visiting Scholar at National University of Singapore and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

My research interests include optimization and machine learning with applications in engineering. I currently focus on

  • Artificial Intelligence for Optimization (AI4OPT)
  • Optimization for Large Language Models (OPT4LLM)
