About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Associate Professor at Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I received my Ph.D in Mathematics from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2019, and then worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Peking University from 2019 to 2021. I was a Visiting Scholar at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

My research interest lies in the intersection of theory and practice, particularly optimization in the era of artificial intelligence. I currently focus on

  • Large Language Models for Operations Research
  • Optimization for Large Language Models
  • Embodied Navigation
