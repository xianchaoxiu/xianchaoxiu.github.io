About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Assistant Professor at Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I obtained my Ph.D in Mathematics from Beijing Jiaotong University, and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University.

My research interest lies in optimization, machine learning, and data science. I currently focus on artificial intelligence for optimization (AI4OPT) with applications in pattern recognition and robotics.
