About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Assistant Professor with the Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I obtained my Ph.D in Operations Reasearch from Beijing Jiaotong University and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University. My current research interests are machine learning (Sparse Optimization, Federated Learning) and pattern recognition (Hyperspectral Imaging, Anomaly Detection).

I am recruiting self-motivated graduate students with great interests in machine learning and pattern recognition. Reference books

  • Optimization: Modeling, Algorithm and Theory (in Chinese) [Book] [Web]
  • Fundamentals of Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning [Book] [Web]
