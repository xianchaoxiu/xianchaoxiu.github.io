About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Assistant Professor with the Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I obtained my PhD in Operations research from Beijing Jiaotong University and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University. My current research interests are numerical optimization (Sparse Optimization, Federated Optimization) and image processing (Hyperspectral Imaging, Interpretable Imaging).

I am recruiting self-motivated graduate students with great interests in machine learning and pattern recognition. Reference books

  • Optimization: Modeling, Algorithm and Theory (in Chinese) [Book] [Web]
  • Fundamentals of Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning [Book] [Web]
