About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Assistant Professor with the Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I obtained my PhD in Operations research from Beijing Jiaotong University and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University. My current research interests are machine learning and optimization (MLO), including Sparse Optimization, Federated Learning, Interpretable Network, Hyperspectral Imaging.

I am recruiting self-motivated graduate students with some mathematical ability. Reference books

  • Machine Learning (in Chinese) [Book]
