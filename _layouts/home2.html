About Me

Dr. Xiu is a Lecturer at Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I obtained my Ph.D in Mathematics from Beijing Jiaotong University, and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University. I was a Visiting Scholar at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University from February 2025 to August 2025.

My research interest lies in the theoretical and algorithmic foundations in optimization, machine learning, and data science. I currently focus on

  • Artificial Intelligence for Optimization (AI4OPT)
  • Optimization for Large Language Models (OPT4LLM)
