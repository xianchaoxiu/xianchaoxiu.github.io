About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Associate Professor at Department of Automation, Shanghai University.

I received my Ph.D in Mathematics from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2019, and then worked as a Postdoctoral Researcher at Peking University from 2019 to 2021. I was a Visiting Scholar at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

My current research interest lies in the intersection of optimization and engineering, particularly

  • Artificial Intelligence for Optimization (AI4OPT)
  • Vision-Language-Action (VLA)
