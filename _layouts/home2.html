About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Assistant Professor at Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I obtained my Ph.D in Operations Research from Beijing Jiaotong University, and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University.

My research interest lies in artificial intelligence and optimization. I currently focus on data-driven optimization with applications in machine learning, pattern recognition, and robotics.
