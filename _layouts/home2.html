About Me

Dr. Xiu is an Assistant Professor at Department of Automation, Shanghai University. I obtained my Ph.D in Operations Research from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2019, and then worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Peking University from 2019 June to 2021 May.

My research interest lies in artificial intelligence and optimization (人工智能与最优化). I am currently focusing on data-driven optimization with applications in machine learning, pattern recognition, and robotics.

I am now recruiting self-motivated students who have certain optimization abilities and a strong interest in machine learning. Reference books

  • Machine Learning [Book] [Video]
